💅 The Gel-X Era: My Current (Healthy?) Obsession

It started innocently enough: A TikTok here, a Pinterest pin there. Suddenly, I was deep in the world of almond shapes, glazed donut finishes, and nail tech terminology I 100% did not understand a week ago.

Now? I’m fully in my Gel-X era — and I’m not even mad about it.

💫 What is Gel-X?

If you’re new here: Gel-X is basically the lovechild of gel polish and extensions. Lightweight, natural-looking, and way gentler on your nails than acrylics. Think press-ons, but make it ✨ bougie ✨.

🧠 Why am I obsessed?

Because honestly? There’s something therapeutic about having your life together at the tip of your fingers. Literally.

I could be spiraling emotionally, but if my nails are done? I’m at peace. I’m delusional, but I’m pretty.

Also, they last. And I get to feel like a walking Pinterest board.

📸 Current Moodboard Vibes:

  • Milky white with gold foil? Yes.
  • Soft chrome almond nails? Double yes.
  • Baby pink jelly tips that scream “I have my life together”? We manifest.

💸 Will this become an expensive habit?

Absolutely.
Do I care?
Not enough to stop.

🫶 Final Thoughts:

There’s something so small yet powerful about investing in tiny rituals that make you feel like you.
For me right now? It’s Gel-X.
Next month it might be lattes or skincare or learning to knit. Who knows?

But for now, I’ll be over here, showing people my nails unprovoked.
If loving them is wrong, I don’t wanna be right.
